Attending Tech related meetups around town!

Even though I am a very social person, the thought of going to meetups in professional setting where I don't know anyone was daunting. I don't know any software engineers and have never been to engineers meetups before.

I signed up for meetup app and started looking for tech related meetups in town. There were many to say and I felt bit overwheled at first; simply because I didn't know where to start and which one to go to. I finally gathered enough courage to attend one of the women who code meetup.

It was in downtown cafe on Sunday morning. As I walked in, there were few people in the cafe. I asked one lady and she told me I was in the right place and mentioned she was the organizer of the meetup. As I introduced myself, I mentioned that I was new to the group and I am here meet people as Iam exploring to change career in Tech. She was very nice and told me that group welcomes everyone and she introduced to everyone in the cafe. Everyone in the group was nice and they shared their experiences as a career in Tech and some of them gave resources to look into as I explore the career.
